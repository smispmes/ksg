About Us

The Security Institute of the Kenya School of Government is committed to excellence in security training, research, and policy development. As a premier center of knowledge, the institute equips security professionals with advanced skills, leadership capacity, and innovative approaches to address emerging challenges in national, regional, and global security. Our mandate is to strengthen security governance through capacity building, professional development, and applied research. The institute collaborates with government agencies, international partners, and communities to promote peace, safety, and resilience. Guided by integrity, professionalism, and service to the nation, the Security Institute is a hub for cultivating future-ready security leaders capable of safeguarding Kenya’s sovereignty and advancing global peace initiatives.
